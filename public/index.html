About our Website

Cocktail-finder is web-resouce where any fan of exotic drinks, mixes of different flavors, or just experimenter can get all the information which may be necessary about spirits, adds, types of drinks etc.

Here you can find information about your favorite drink and its preparation method, learn more about options of cocktails with certain ingredients, and choose a suitable recipe.

Our page represents a collection of the greatest world-known cocktails, and there is the possibility of using the election of ingredients that are available to you at the moment to determine for which of the drinks you have everything necessary today.
